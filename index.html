Teaching & Research

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh. My research interests lie in Applied Cryptography, Information Security, and Privacy Preserving Information Retrieval. I completed my MSc at KUET with a perfect CGPA of 4.00, focusing on secure multi-party computation. I have authored publications in IEEE journals and conferences, and serve as a reviewer for international venues. Beyond research and teaching, I am passionate about painting — a creative outlet that complements my analytical work.
